About Me

I am a Lead Full-Stack Software Engineer building scalable mobile and web systems, with a growing focus on Applied AI Engineering and cybersecurity. My work explores how intelligent systems can be integrated into production software while maintaining strong guarantees around security, performance, and reliability. I am particularly interested in designing systems that scale in practice, remain secure under real-world constraints, and meaningfully improve how people interact with technology.
